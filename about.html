About

Live tracking apps have made a significant positive impact on how easy our lives have become. A user-friendly tracking system called MetroYathra enables users to view train arrival and departure times, metro schedules, and much more. The number of individuals switching to the metro in Kochi for a quick, punctual, and dependable intra-city transit is steadily rising. Finding the trains is much simpler with MetroYathra at your fingertips.
